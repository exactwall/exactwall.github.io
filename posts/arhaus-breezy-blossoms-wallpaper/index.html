  1. Traditional Stick Wallpaper: This type of wallpaper is made of paper and has a water-activated adhesive backing. It is the most common type of wallpaper and is suitable for most walls.

  2. Self-Adhesive Wallpaper: This type of wallpaper has an adhesive backing that does not require water activation. It is ideal for use on walls that are uneven or textured.
