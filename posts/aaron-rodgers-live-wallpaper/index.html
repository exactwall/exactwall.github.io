How to Remove Peel Wallpaper Removing peel wallpaper is a project that can be completed in a few hours. With the right tools and supplies, you can easily remove peel wallpaper from your walls. Here are a few tips on how to remove peel wallpaper:

  1. Start by scoring the wallpaper with a scoring tool. This will help create small holes in the wallpaper so that water can penetrate it.

  2. Next, soak a sponge in warm water and apply it to the scored area of the wallpaper.

  3. Allow the water to soak into the paper for several minutes before attempting to remove the paper.

  4. Use a putty knife or other sharp object to slowly peel away the wet wallpaper.

  5. Repeat steps 2-4 until all of the peel wallpaper has been removed from your walls.
